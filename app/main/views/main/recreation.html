<:Title> Recreation - Dan Reynolds <:Body>

Outside is fun!

I've been an avid sports player since I was young. When I can, I like to go out and ride my mountain bike, or throwing a frisbee, sometimes both. I've been trail riding for many years and would really like to get out more. I currently have a 29er single speed that is looking to get some winter love. Give me more than five minutes notice and I'll meet you out there. Frisbee is my other passion. We finished our intramural league for the fall and everyone had a blast. I am looking forward to the spring when we can get back at it.
